# Getting Started

Get up and running with your first virtual machine in just a few steps.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up using email/password or Google OAuth for quick access to the platform.

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Navigate to **My Account** section and complete your profile with billing information (required for payments).

**Required information:**
- Address
- Postal code
- Country

## Step 3: Add Credits

Purchase credits in the **Credits** section to pay for your virtual machines and storage.

**Minimum amounts:**
- USD: $1.00
- EUR: €1.00  
- INR: ₹100.00

## Step 4: Setup SSH Access

Create or import an SSH key from the **SSH Keys** section for secure VM access.

## Step 5: Deploy Your VM

Use the **Deploy VM** wizard to launch your first virtual machine.

Choose from:
- **GPU Type:** H100, A100, L40, RTX A6000, or A40
- **Region:** Norway, Canada, or US
- **Operating System:** Ubuntu with CUDA pre-installed

## What's Next?

After your VM is deployed:
1. Connect via SSH
2. Configure firewall rules
3. Create storage volumes
4. Take snapshots for backup
